      pst

      clojure.repl

      Available since 1.3
      • (pst)
      • (pst e-or-depth)
      • (pst e depth)
      Prints a stack trace of the exception, to the depth requested. If none supplied, uses the root cause of the
most recent repl exception (*e), and a depth of 12.
